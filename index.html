Who We Are

BORN TO
RIDE WILD

Roadiez Adventure Club is Pune's most thrilling off-road motorcycle racing community. We bring together riders who share a passion for speed, adrenaline, and the open road. From hill sprints to desert expeditions, we live for the thrill.

Founded by riders, for riders — every event is crafted to push limits, build brotherhood, and create memories that last a lifetime.
